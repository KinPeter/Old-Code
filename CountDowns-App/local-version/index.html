Welcome!


Using this webapp you can save countdown timers (like the demo below) for anything that you are really waiting for. Here I'm gonna give you some instructions how to do so.

First, please note that all data is stored locally, so your user account and timers will persist only until you clear the data of your browser. Also the data is bound to this browser only, that you are using now.

Creating an account and logging in: To create your account, just click on the Sign Up link above, and enter your name, email and a strong password. Afterwards you can log in using your credentials by clicking on Log In. The app will keep you logged in for 2 days.

Creating timers: Once you logged in you will see a Create New Timer link on the navbar, so you can set new timers right away. If your timer is expired, or you just don't like it anymore, you can remove it by clicking the small × symbol on the right.

In case you don't want to use the app anymore, you can always delete your account by clicking on Account > Delete my account button. This will also remove all timers set for your account.
